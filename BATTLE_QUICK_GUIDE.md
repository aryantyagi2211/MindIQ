# 1v1 Battle System - Quick Guide

## 🎯 What's New?

### 1. Online Players Counter
- **Live Player Count**: See how many players are currently online in battles
- Updates every 5 seconds automatically
- Shows only active players (not offline)

### 2. Enhanced Matchmaking
- **Player Stat Cards**: See opponent's score and field before battle
- **Your Stats**: Your latest test score is displayed
- **Better Visuals**: Gradient cards with animations

### 3. Battle System (How It Works)
- ✅ Both players get the **same questions**
- ✅ Answer independently at your own pace
- ✅ Winner determined automatically after both finish
- ✅ Detailed breakdown of all answers

---

## 🎮 How to Battle

### Step 1: Start a Battle
1. Go to Battle Arena section
2. Check how many players are online
3. Select your field (e.g., Technology)
4. Select your subfield (e.g., Web Development)
5. Click "FIND OPPONENT"

### Step 2: Matchmaking
- See live online player count
- Wait for an opponent (max 30 seconds)
- View opponent's stat card when matched
- Questions are generated automatically

### Step 3: Fight!
- Answer 10 questions
- Each question has a time limit
- Progress bar shows your position
- Timer counts down for each question

### Step 4: Results
- Wait for opponent to finish (if you're first)
- See final scores for both players
- Winner gets a crown 👑
- Review all questions and answers

## 🔥 Pro Tips

1. **Check Stats First**: See how many players are online before starting
2. **Know Your Opponent**: Review their score during matchmaking
3. **Time Management**: Each question has a countdown timer
4. **Stay Focused**: You can't go back to previous questions
5. **Fair Play**: Both players get identical questions

---

## ⚡ Quick Facts

- **Timeout**: 30 seconds to find an opponent
- **Questions**: 10 per battle
- **Scoring**: Percentage-based (0-100%)
- **Winner**: Highest score wins
- **Draw**: Possible if scores are equal
- **Real-time**: Live updates during matchmaking
