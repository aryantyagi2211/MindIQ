## 🎯 How to Use

### Step 1: Browse Fields
- Click on any field tab
- See player count on each tab
- Choose popular fields for faster matching

### Step 2: Select Specialization
- Pick your area of expertise
- Or choose "All Categories" for Mastermind

### Step 3: Find Opponent
- Click "FIND OPPONENT"
- Match with players in same field
- Start your battle!
